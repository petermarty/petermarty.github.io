Building A Brand for All

The objective at Peter Marty Design is straightforward: Provide high quality solutions to like-minded people while building meaningful partnerships. To communicate this we created a content-first website with an information architecture that is easy to navigate. We wrapped everything in a modern, elegant design.

Device Agnostic Journey Mapping

To ensure the effective execution of a seamless solution accross all devices, we included it all: strategic planning and research; focussed user testing, interaction design, visual design and branding; resulting in a highly optimized user interface, with a dynamically extensible back end.

Laying out a Roadmap

A thorough roadmapping exercise was highly beneficial to this project's success. We started by identifying everything we wanted to be included, as well as everything that was nice to have. This high level roadmap was a success because we left room to adjust the iterative details as the project progressed forward.

User-Centered Research

We looked at our business under a microscope to create user personas and formulate user interviews. This research allowed for informed wireframes and a dynamic prototype for our user testing to reveal any potential design oversights before moving on to visual design.

Streamlined Development

In line with our user‐centered approach we developed a custom coded, highly optimized experience. We utilize a static site generator allowing us to avoid time consuming server calls to get the site's content; utilizing cutting edge technology for an optimal user experience.

Constant Iteration

A large part of our job is studying human behavior. One thing that is a constant is a design can always be refined through future iteration. We ensure successful design iteration through watching you, (creepy right?), interact with and use the site through precise analytical data.
