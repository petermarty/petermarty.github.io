Synopsis

Tucked away on the banks of the Mississippi river in the Northeast Minneapolis' warehouse district, Sip Coffeebar is a cozy little shop with a modern touch. They requested an updated, mobile friendly, user focused web presence that matched the feel of their physical location. We set out to accomplish this through user interviews, wireframing, designing, prototyping, user testing, and developing a custom developed Wordpress solution that was fully manageable and maintainable into the future of Sipcoffebar as the business grows.
